10 Best Wireless Earbuds for Everyone

Wireless earbuds used to be like a dream few years back. Listening to music or talking to someone by popping a tiny little headphone into each ear without any sting attached. Previously the concept of wirefree earbuds were different. Most of the wireless earbuds were huge in size, very low battery support and tons of other problems. Now things have changed and we have lots of well-furnished wireless earbuds that works like never before. Here are some suggestions of wireless earbuds that you should try with a wide varieties of price and design. Choose which you prefer the most.

1. 1More ColorBuds

ColorBuds from the audio brand 1More are super comfortable and sweat-resistant. The price is around 100$ and the sound quality is better than those of having twice the price. Because of their silicon ring-tips you can use them for a long time without even realizing that they’re even there. They got an IPX5 – Protects from water jets at any direction. This wireless earbuds have 4 built in mics for better call quality.

2.Bose QuietComfort Earbuds

Bose’s brand new earbuds are one of the best noise cancelling earbuds. They are also amazing in many ways. They are bit larger than most available earbuds but these extra-large space are used for a series of microphone and processors which are used for canceling the noise from outside world.

They provide an AirPod Pro which include 6 hours of battery life. Quick charge is also available via USB TYPE-C. They allow you two hours of charge in only 15 minutes. It’s really suitable for working out. It has an IPX4 – Protects from splashing water, no matter the direction.

3.JLab Audio Go Air

This is one of best low budget earbud. Everyone can’t afford 100+ dollars in wireless earbuds. JLab has done a fascinating job with this earbud which is around 30$. They provide 5 hours battery life, super comfortable to use and also sweat proof. Some drawbacks of this earbuds are they’re heavier on the base compared to other earbuds and the case of the earbud easily attracts dusts so it required frequent cleaning.

4.Apple AirPods Pro

This is one of the expensive wireless earbuds from Apple. They offer only 4.5 hours of battery life. They are the most suitable headphone for the Apple users. Apple used H1 chips which offers instant pairing, noise cancellation which remove unnecessary sound and also provide silicon air trips which make them comfortable to use.

Say “Hi Siri” and Apple’s voice assistant will be activated and assist you to change songs, send text to someone or add anything to the shopping list. They have an IPX4 – Protects from splashing water, no matter the direction. Apple AirPods Pro case also supports wireless charging.

MW07 Go is water resistant and it has slotted silicone ear fins which makes is very comfortable to use and functions better than more expensive workout earbuds. They have pause/play button at the top of the earbuds so it’s very easy to use in any situation.
