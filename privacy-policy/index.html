Cookies

Cookie refers to a very little text documents. It carries an identifier which is anonymous and unique. After your visit to a website, that site asks permission to save this unique file to your hard drive. A website is allowed to send cookies to your device only if your browser allow it.

IP Addresses

Your computer use the IP address when you connect to the internet. It is used by the machines on the network to identify your device. Web servers automatically collect this IP address.
