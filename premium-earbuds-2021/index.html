10 Wireless Premium Earbuds 2021

Last Updated on : January 4,2021

Wireless premium earbuds used to be like a dream few years back. Listening to music or talking to someone by popping a tiny little headphone into each ear without any sting attached.

Previously the concept of wireless premium earbuds were different. Most of the wireless headphones were bulky in size, very low battery support and tons of other problems. Now things have changed and we have lots of well-furnished and high quality wireless earbuds that works like never before. Here are some suggestions of wireless premium earbuds that you should try in this new year of 2021 with a wide varieties of price and design.

Each of our pics are very popular, light weight and premium in their own way. Choose which you prefer the most.

Samsung Galaxy Buds Live

Undoubtedly the most premium wireless earbuds in the list is Samsung Galaxy Buds Live with their unique look. They are semi sealed. These premium earbuds have impressive noise canceling feature and the shape of the earbuds looks more like beans. So many prefer to call them “Bean Buds”.

Attractive looking Galaxy Buds Live have touch sensors of single, double and triple tap which serves play/pause, skipping sound track forward or backward. Having Spotify integration is also a unique quality of premium Galaxy Buds.

They provide comfortable fit, premium sound and call quality. They have IPX2 water and sweat resistant rating. They don’t have the traditional silicon ear trips. They provide 6 hours of battery life with a single charge and the case allows you two more full charges. These premium earbuds also capable of wireless charging. They are pretty good low budget alternative of Apple’s AirPods Pro premium earbuds.

Samsung Galaxy Buds Plus

This is one of the low budget but premium earbuds from Samsung. The design of Galaxy Buds Plus are very fashionable. They provide comfortable fit and very easy to use. These premium earbuds have IPX2 water resistant and sweat resistant rating. Spotify integration is also included with these premium earbuds.

Samsung Galaxy Buds Plus comes with active noise canceling feature and 11 hours of battery backup with just a single charge. One more full charge with the charging case. These premium earbuds in 3 minutes of charging provides up to one hour of backup. Sound, call and microphone quality is super amazing and also supports wireless charging.

Jabra Elite Active 75T

Jabra’s latest premium earbuds Elite Active 75T comes with a rating of IP57 which means it is completely waterproof and sweat proof. So, they are one of the best wireless premium earbuds for workout, outdoor work, long run and any sort of social distanced tour. Removing one earbud automatically pauses the track and reinserting resumes the track.

Jabra Elite Active 75T have a premium look and very amazing sound and call quality. These stylish premium earbuds have active noise cancelling feature. They got battery life of about 7.5 hours and additional 22 hours with the case. A small drawback is that they are a bit large for comparatively small ears.

Bose QuietComfort Earbuds

Bose’s brand new premium earbuds are “The King” of active noise cancelling earbuds. They are also amazing in many ways. They are bit larger than most available earbuds but these extra-large space are used for a series of microphone and processors which are used impressively for canceling the noise from outside world.

Bose QuietComfort premium earbuds provide 6 hours of battery backup with a single charge. Two additional full charge form the case. Quick charge is also available via USB TYPE-C. They allow you two hours of backup in only 15 minutes of charge. These premium earbuds are really suitable for working out. They have an IPX4 water and sweat resistant rating which means they allow protection from splashing water, no matter the direction.

JLab Audio Go Air

JLab Audio Go Air are cheap but premium earbuds. Everyone can’t afford 100+ dollars in wireless earbuds. JLab has done a fascinating job with these premium earbuds which are only 30$. They are supreme alternative to many of the expensive earbuds. They provide 5 hours battery life in a single charge. Additional 15 hours with the case.

These premium earbuds are super comfortable to use. They are certified with IP44 rating means they are minor splash and sweat resistant. Some drawbacks of these earbuds are, they’re heavier on the base compared to other earbuds (recommended for base lovers) and the case of the earbuds easily attracts dusts so it required frequent cleaning.

Master & Dynamic MW07 Go

One of the best suitable for workout wireless premium earduds are Master & Dynamic MW07 Go. They offer amazing sound quality with a long lasting battery life of about 10 hours, 12 more hours of backup with the case.

MW07 Go premium earbuds are IPX6 certified which means they are perfectly water and sweat resistant. They have slotted silicone ear fins which make them very comfortable to use and functions better than more expensive workout premium earbuds. They have pause/play button at the top of the earbuds so they are very easy to use in any situation.

House of Marley Liberate Air

House of Marley Liberate Air are the most ecofriendly premium earbuds out there. House of Marley claims that they have semi-sustainable constructions. The buds and the case are built of sustainable sourced bamboo, recycled aluminum and a fabric made of recycled plastic bottles. Though Liberate Air used old materials, it looks really stylish.

These premium earbuds provides 9 hours of battery life and 23 hours more with the case. These earbuds are well fit with decent sound and call quality. They allow powerful bass audio experience. Some drawbacks are, sometimes pick up noise and not very much repairable.

Apple AirPods Pro

This is one of the expensive wireless premium earbuds from Apple. They offer only 4.5 hours of battery life. They are the most suitable headphone for the Apple users. Apple used H1 chips which offers instant pairing, noise cancellation which remove unnecessary sound and also provide silicon air trips which make them comfortable to use.

Say “Hi Siri” and Apple’s voice assistant will be activated and assist you to change songs, send text to someone or add anything to the shopping list. These premium earbuds have an IPX4 water resistant rating – which means it is protected from splashing water, no matter the direction. Case of Apple AirPods Pro also supports wireless charging.

Beats Powerbeats Pro

Apple’s Beats Powerbeats Pro are the most premium earbuds than any other earbuds in this list. They will give a super secure fit in any sort of workout. No matter how sweatiest it gets, they will stay protected because they have an IPX4 water and sweat resistant rating – which means protected from splashing water, no matter the direction.
