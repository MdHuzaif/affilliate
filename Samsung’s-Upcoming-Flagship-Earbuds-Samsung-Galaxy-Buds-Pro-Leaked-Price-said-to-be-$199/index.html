Samsung’s Upcoming Flagship Earbuds Samsung Galaxy Buds Pro Leaked Price said to be $199

The pictures of Samsung’s upcoming Active Noise Cancelling earbuds leaked earlier this month. As the leak continues, now we got a new leak about the cost of this upcoming flagship earbuds.

Famous twitter leaker WalkingCat posted a comparison chart related to the price of this new Samsung Galaxy Buds Pro along with previous Samsung Galaxy Buds Plus and Samsung Galaxy Buds Live. The chart shows that the price of this new buds pro will be $199. Which is 30$ more than previous Samsung Galaxy Buds Live at $169 and 50$ more than Samsung Galaxy Buds Plus at $149.

Samsung Galaxy Buds Pro will have “Immersive sound” along with “intelligent active noise cancelling”. These new buds will also have an impressive water and sweat resistant rating of IPX7, which is the best water resistant rating an earbuds can achieve. They can stay under 1 meter of water for up to 30 minutes. Water resistant rating of previous both buds pro and buds live are IPX2 which is below average.

According to the leaked photos the design of upcoming Galaxy Buds Pro is similar to the Galaxy Buds Plus. There is bit difference between the battery backup. Galaxy Buds Pro will provide 8 hours of battery backup with a single charge but the Galaxy Buds Plus allows 11 hours of backup. The upcoming buds pro will lack behind 3 hours of backup on the buds comparing with previous Buds Plus.

The new Galaxy Buds Pro will have an advantage over Galaxy Buds Plus on the overall playback time with the case. Upcoming Galaxy Buds Pro will provide 28 hours of playback time with the case but Galaxy Buds Plus only allow 22 hours playback with the case.

Upcoming Galaxy Buds Pro will provide only 4.5 hours of talk time on the buds and 15 hours talk time with case. Which is a bit less than the previous Buds Plus and Buds Live. Galaxy Buds Plus provide 7.5 hours talk time with the buds 3 hours more than new buds pro and 15 hours with the case which is same as Buds pro.

WalkingCat’s another leak picture shows that Samsung Galaxy Buds Pro will have features of 11mm woofer as well as 6.5mm twitter drivers also having special sound features which are petty similar to Apple’s Airpods Pro.

The 199$ price of Galaxy Buds Pro seems very competitive in the market. 50$ less than Apple’s Airpods Pro. According to the features and sound quality they seems to provide similar performance of Apples Airpods pro and Bose QuietComfort earbuds at a comparatively lower price.

The new Samsung’s premium Galaxy Buds Pro will launch along with the Galaxy S21 smartphone lineup on the January 2021 Galaxy Unpacked event.
