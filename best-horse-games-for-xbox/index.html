Best Horse Games for Xbox

Xbox is known as a video gaming brand which is created and owned by Microsoft. Xbox consists of 5 video gaming consoles, also produce video games, streaming services and Xbox live online services. Xbox is one of the most popular gaming platform now a days. Xbox one console is mostly preferred by the gamers. Brand new Xbox x/s series released on November 2020 which is the latest version of Xbox console.

At present horse games have become very popular among the Xbox One users. The reason behind the popularity of Xbox horse games is that they are based on action and adventure. Everybody likes wild life adventures with horse ride which also creates a 20th century vibe. Horse games of Xbox One includes horse riding, hunting, shootouts, heists, fighting, armor shield etc. which makes them more thrilling to the gamers.

If you are an Xbox one user or willing to buy one and also fascinated to play horse games on Xbox then you have come to the perfect place. We have compiled a comprehensive list only for you of best horse games that you need to play on your Xbox now, and we will continue to add more games to keep it up to date. Some of the games on the list revolve around horses and the others only allow horse riding. We can assure you that all the games on our list are very amusing and loved by thousands of Xbox one users.


Red Dead Redemption 2

Red Dead Redemption 2 is published by the Rockstar Gamers in 2018 which is based on action and adventure. The game is “Red Dead” series third entry and also contains the backstory of previous Read Dead Redemption game which was released on 2010. The story of the game is about Southern United States, Midwestern and Western fictionalized representation in 1899.

The main character of the game is named Arthur Morgan who is a member of outlaw gang Van der Linde. As the decline of the Wild West is upon Arthur and his gang so they have to deal with it at the same time try to survive from rival gangs, government forces and other adversaries. The protagonist of Red Dead Redemption John Marston who is a fellow gang member is also followed by the story.

The player of the game can easily move around the open world interactive gameplay elements. They can take part in interactive horseback riding, heist, shootouts, hunting, interacting with non-player characters and maintain characters honor rating. The main transportation of this game are horses. The players of the game can easily steal a horse and they have to train or tame the horse before using it.

As we can see there is a lot of interaction with horses in Red Dead Redemption 2 which makes it very amusing for horse game lovers. Traveling by horses, shooting, hunting most of the activities of the game revolve around horses. Horses are the main transportation system as well as a vital role playing element of the game. So we believe this famous Red Dead Redemption 2 is the best horse game for Xbox till now.

The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt

Witcher 3: Wild Hunt is another Xbox One horse related game which is an action role playing game of 2015 developed and published by CD Projekt Red . The story of Witcher 3 is based on Andrzej Sapkowski fantasy novels named “The Witcher”. The game is a third main installment of the Witcher series which is a sequel of previous Witcher 2 of 2011. We can play the game as third person perspective in open world.

The main character of the game is Geralt of Rivia who is a hired monster slayer also known as a Witcher in a fictional fantasy world which is based on Slavonic mythology. Witcher is on quest of his adopted daughter who is running away from otherworldly wild hunt. The game is very amusing and adventurous. Players can battle in the game with magic and weapons, complete quests, non-player character interaction, acquire points and gold, purchase equipment and increase Geralt’s abilities.

Geralt can move around the open world region riding on his horse named Roach. Players can kill enemies while riding Roach. Other activities of the game include horse racing, card playing, boxing. The combination between Geralt and his horse Roach is going to be very fascinating for the horse game lovers of Xbox.

Red Dead Redemption

Red Dead Redemption is a popular Xbox horse game full of action and adventure published by Rockstar Games in 2010. This is the second game of Red Dead series. The first one was Red Dead Revolver published in 2004. The story of the game is about 1911 during the time of American frontier. The game is available for both Xbox One and Xbox 360 users.

The main character of the game is former outlaw John Marston. His wife and son are taken hostage by the government. They want his service as a hired gun in ransom. Marston don’t have a choice but to do as they say. So he sets in order to bring three members of his former gang to justice.

Here also the player can move around the interactive open world which is a fictionalized version of Mexico and Western United States. The players can move around on horseback. There is also morality system which maintains the character’s level of honor and fame.

Same as Red Dead Redemption 2 this Xbox game also provide the main form of transportation as horse. Players in order to use horses, must tame horses in the wild or steal them. One of game’s greatest success is the relationship between the player and their horse. Horseback shootouts will also provide a very thrilling experience to the gamers. Horse being a part and parcel of this game makes it one of the best horse focused game for Xbox One and also Xbox 360 users.

Farming Simulator 19

Farming Simulator 19 is a very famous game also a farming simulation video game published by Focus Home Interactive in November 2018. This amusing farming game is also an Xbox One horse game. The locations of this game is based on Americans and European environments. The players can enjoy this farming game by growing crops, breading livestock, farming and selling assets created by farming.

With the overwhelming and impressive graphics Farming Simulator 19 will allow you to grow your own virtual farm. Players will be able to use various farming tools, machines and vehicles. Unlike the previous versions of Farming Simulator this version newly introduce horse farming, also oat and cotton crops farming. The players can livestock horses as well as other domestic animals. As Farming Simulator 19 include horses in the game through horse faming, horse riding and also provide an opportunity to interact with horses so we decide to list it as a decent horse included game for the Xbox One horse fans.

The Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim

The Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim is a role playing open world Xbox One horse related game published by Bethesda Softworks. This is the 5th game from “The Elder Scrolls” series. This game is also very popular and amusing among Xbox One users.

The main character of the game is Dragonborn, the main story revolve around this player. They are on a quest to defeat the World-Eater Alduin. The game include prophesy of Alduin the dragon to destroy the world. The game take place in Skyrim which is the northernmost province of Tamriel. The development of the character and improvement of skill is done by the player through completion of quests which are very amusing.

This game also include the open world system which means the player can move to anywhere they want on the land of Skyrim and play the way they like without following the storyline. The environment consists of dungeons, caves, cities, towns, fortresses wilderness expanses and villages. The players can travel in all these places using their horses.

The Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim also include weapons, armor, magic spells, shields, wild creatures, powerful spells from dragon soul. The combination of weapons and magical combat against powerful dragon provides a very thrilling experience. As players can use horses to move around and fight against the enemy so undoubtedly this is an amusing horse riding games for Xbox.
